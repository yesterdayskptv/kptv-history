In the Garden with Mike Darcy
March 11, 2000 - August 31, 2002


 Mike Darcy, long-time host of a gardening show on KXL radio, came to television as host of KPTV's In the Garden with Mike Darcy.

After three successful seasons, Mike moved his show to KATU (2) prior to the merging of KPTV and KPDX in September 2002.  The garden show continued on KPTV as part of a new program, Good Day Lifestyles, which also featured "home" and "food" segments.
