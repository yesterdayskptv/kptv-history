Sports Clinic
February 20, 1954 - July 3, 1954
 

Host Bill Stout welcomed a prominent guest from the local sporting world on this weekend show. A panel of youngsters would ask questions of the guest, who would demonstrate his answers. Featured were skiers, baseball players, football players, high school and college coaches and other famous figures of the sports world.
