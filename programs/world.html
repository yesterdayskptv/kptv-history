The World on View
1953?


Left to right: Gene Brendler, Bill Stout and George Sage, on the 
"World on View" set.

A 10-minute news show, with on the spot pictures, plus coverage of national and international news, sports and weather. Gene Brendler read the news, Bill Stout handled sports and George Sage covered the weather.


Stout, Brendler and Sage prepare for another broadcast.
