Treasure Hunt
 October 10, 1953 - January 30, 1954
 

Left to right: Cretoria Thompson, Bob McCarl, Pat Carmen and
Bob McAnulty go hunting for treasure.

  

Treasure Hunt, a new, locally produced "live" comedy-quiz show with panel members may be seen Saturday over KPTV at 6:30pm.

The panel consists of four members well known to Portlanders.  They are: Cretoria Thompson, known better as just "Cretoria, reducing expert" and columnist for the Oregon Journal; Pat Carmen, model director at the Maria Easterly School of Modeling; T. Lawson McCall, "dean of the Portland news commentators" and Bob McAnulty, Portland disc jockey on station KWJJ.

The program is moderated by Bob McCarl, disc jockey on station KLIQ and will be telecast live from the studios of KPTV.  Each week there will be three guests on the show.  There will also be facilities for studio audience and the public is invited to attend telecasts.

"Treasure Hunt" is a Dial production.
