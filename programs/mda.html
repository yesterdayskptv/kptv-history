This was the last MDA Telethon to air on KPTV. In 2003, the show moved to KPDX (49) to allow "Good Day Oregon" to broadcast on Labor Day.

In addition to the usual telethon events, a tribute was given to "Rambin' Rod" Anders, who had hosted the telethon on KPTV for 31 years, and who had died in May 2002.
