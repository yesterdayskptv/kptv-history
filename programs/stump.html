Stump the DJs
+/- November 11, 1955 - January 1956 + ?
March 31/April 7, 1962 - May 12, 1962

 

Moderator Maggi Ramsey consults with "Mystery Guest" Jack
Hurd, editor of Portland Radio and TV Guide, as they try to
"Stump the DJs."

 

Early live and local quiz show, tried to get the better of local disc jockeys by testing their musical knowledge.

In the 1962 version, Barney Keep, Sammy Taylor, Bill Davis, Addie Bobkins and Bob Blackburn tried to identify a mystery tune and a mystery guest.
