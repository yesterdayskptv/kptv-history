Heck Harper's Jamboree
March 11, 1956 - September 7, 1956
 

 

Heck Harper (far right) with the trio on his show.
Left to right: Judy Sands, Lee Knight and
Harley Hess.

 

Popular children's host Heck Harper also hosted this show, which presented live western music to Portland viewers.
