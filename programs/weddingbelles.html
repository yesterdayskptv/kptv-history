Wedding Belles
August 9 1953 - November 6, 1955 + ?


Host Gene Brendler poses in front of the "Wedding Belles"
logo.

Brad Reynolds (August 9 - 23, 1953) and Gene Brendler (August 30 and later) hosted this show for brides-to-be, with helpful hints by a panel of wedding experts on such problems as going away clothes, decorations and receptions. A fashion show, of fashions for the bride and her mother, was another feature of this program, sponsored by Bruno Photography Studios.
