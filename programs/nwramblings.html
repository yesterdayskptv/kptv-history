Northwest Ramblings
September 28, 1957 - November 1958 + ?

Bob Brown hosted this program which featured places of local interest.

 

NORTHWEST RAMBLINGS - SELECTED PROGRAM LISTINGS

December 14, 1957
"The Story of the Nestucca River."  A filmed account of this famous Oregon fishing stream from its head waters down to where it joins the sea.

February 22, 1958
"The Buffalo Mine."  Bob Brown shows films of a hard-rock coal mine operating throughout the winter in the rugged back country of Bend County.
