For Teenagers Only
February 17, 1962 - May 26, 1962+?
 

 
Gene Brendler and Jimmy Jones (right, rear) in the KPTV
studios "For Teenagers Only."		  

Gene Brendler and Jimmy Jones hosted this program, designed for and aimed at the teenage group.  The show was planned around teenage activities and covered all local events of interest to high school students.  A weekly feature was a salute to an Oregon high school.

Some local events featured on the show included Grant High's production of "Oklahoma" (2/24/62); the Benson High Tech show (2/24/62); the Orchestra Band Festival (3/10/62); and the KBPS student radio station in Benson High School (3/17/62).
