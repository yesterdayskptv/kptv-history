Good Day Oregon
 (August 26, 1996 - present)


Opening titles from the show, 1996.

Oregon's first completely local three-hour morning news program. Designed to compete with NBC's "Today" and ABC's "Good Morning America," "Good Day Oregon" soon established a loyal audience.
