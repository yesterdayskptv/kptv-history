Columbia Crossroads
September 1970 - May 21, 1983
 

Program slide, used in station identifications.

  

A long-running public affairs program which discussed a different topic of local interest with each program. Over the years, many of KPTV's on-air personalities hosted the show, which ran in both morning and evening time-slots each weekend.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


Each program discussed a different topic. This one, from 1983,
covered "The Future of Portland."

SELECTED PROGRAM LISTINGS

January 10, 1971
"Is It Legal?"  A panel of lawyers answers questions telephoned in by viewers on personal liability, domestic relations, criminal law and other phases of law interpretation.  Moderator: Gene Brendler. [LIVE]
