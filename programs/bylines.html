Northwest Bylines
 1980s - 1992

Gene Brendler hosted this compilation of news stories that focused on the Pacific Northwest. Scheduled irregularly during the 1970s, 1980s and early 1990s, it was used primarily to fill time after movies.

In the 1970s and early 1980s, the segments were named
Newsweek Bylines.
