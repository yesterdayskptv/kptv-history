Outdoor Byline
?-April 1957 - May 1957-?

Outdoor Byline host Bob Brown.

KPTV's Outdoor Editor, Bob Brown, brought viewers the latest fishing, camping and boating news, along with sports interviews and outdoor film, in this program which was broadcast live from the KPTV studios.

Some of the topics featured during the shows run: Lake and stream films of the opening week of trout season in Oregon and sequences of the smelt run on the Sandy River.
