KPTV Specials

Through the years, KPTV created many  programs that aired on a limited or "OTO" (one time only) basis.  Either produced in the studio, or from another location with the aid of the KPTV Remote Truck, these programs offered viewers a glimpse into special events, topics of local interest or regional sports.

 

1960    
08/06/60 9:00PM-9:30PM FASHION SHOW
Fall fashions for women and back-to-school clothes for the younger set will be previewed during this special telecast.  The models and clothes will come from Portland's leading department stores.  John Salisbury and Mary Marsh will co-host the fashion parade.
     
1966    
12/21/66 9:30PM-10:30PM BUCKAROO HOCKEY SHOW
Jimmy Jones narrates films of this season's Buckaroo hockey games.
1967    
07/29/67 1:30PM-3:00PM LITTLE LEAGUE BASEBALL
Jimmy Jones reports the regional Little League playoffs for Portland. [LIVE]
1993    
03/28/93 BEYOND EDEN'S GATE: THE LEGACY OF THE OREGON TRAIL (ENCORE)
 A one-hour documentary hosted by Jack McGowan featured such stores as the trails impact on Native Americans, how it forever changed the area's landscape, its effect on individual lives and its significance for the region's economy.
06/05/93   1993 ROSE FESTIVAL STARLIGHT PARADE
Live coverage of the annual spectacle from downtown Portland.
07/18/93 MAGIC IN THE AIR '93
Taped coverage of the 1993 U.S. Bank Rose Festival Airshow. Hosted by Rod Anders and Elaine Murphy, with reporters Lars Larson and Phil Cassidy.
07/24/93 MAGIC IN THE AIR '93 (ENCORE)
10/24/93   OREGON SKIING: THE CUTTING EDGE
 A one-hour look at facilities of Oregon's #1 winter tourist attraction, from Mt. Hood and Mt. Bachelor to family run slopes like Cooper Spur. Hosted by KPTV sports director Michael O'Brien.
12/12/93   OREGON SKIING: THE CUTTING EDGE (ENCORE)
12/26/93 AT JOURNEY'S END: LIFE AFTER THE OREGON TRAIL
 The second part of a look at the 150th celebration of the Oregon Trail. This documentary provided glimpses into the Spartan daily lives of the homesteaders, the cultural diversity of their surroundings and the issues they faced in organizing new communities once the reached the end of the trail.
1994    
06/04/94 1994 ROSE FESTIVAL STARLIGHT PARADE
2002    
09/22/02 6:00PM - 7:00PM KPTV 50TH ANIVERSARY SPECIAL
A one-hour special hosted by former KPTV weatherman David Apple, which covered the first half-century of Oregon's first television station. The program, which interviewed several long-time KPTV employees (many of whom had been laid-off by the time the special aired), was the last program to be completely produced at KPTV's East Portland facilities.
