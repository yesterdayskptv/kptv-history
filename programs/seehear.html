See Hear
 September 27, 1954 - January 6, 1956

"A four-star cast with 25 minutes of top-notch early evening variety entertainment" is how an early sales ad described this program. Song stylist Patty Baldwin, formerly with Ray Anthony's world famed-band joined Al Davis at the piano keyboard and Charles Fendall, one of the Northwest's finest organists. Rounding out the group was KPTV stalwart, Gene Brendler, who was always ready with an intriguing "logic" joke and acted as master of ceremonies. The program presented light musical entertainment as well as some zany nonsense, quick capsules of unusual information and out-of-the-ordinary camera sequences.

Mary Lou Martin was also featured as the vocalist during the show's run.
