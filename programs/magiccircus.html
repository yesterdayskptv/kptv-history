Magic Circus
February 6, 1954 - May 1, 1954

William Desmond starred as Klinker the Clown, a lively and entertaining magician-clown. Desmond was known on the West Coast for his acts of dexterity and sleight-of-hand. As a professional, Desmond played on stage, in radio, television and in clubs.
