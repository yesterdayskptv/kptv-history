Tony Martinez

Tony Martinez is an orignal member of the Good Day Oregon reporter team. Before joining KPTV Oregon's 12, Tony spent 15 years working in radio. Most weekday mornings, you can find Tony, photographer Daryl Mitchell, and a pilot from Hillsboro Aviation flying over the morning commuters in a Bell Jet Ranger 206 helicopter better known as "Air 12." Tony's unique vantage on the morning rush allows him to submit the best traffic reports in the city every ten minutes back to back with Andy Carson's weather reports on the "fives." (6:05, 6:15, 6:25...)

Tony reports the answer to the two questions he hears most often are: "Yes, it's a blast to fly in the helicopter every morning!" and "No, you can't go for a ride."

Tony, his wife Sukhi, their baby daughter, and their Pharoah Hound, Cleo live in Portland. Tony holds a Master's degree in Social Work from Portland State University and works part-time in social service.
