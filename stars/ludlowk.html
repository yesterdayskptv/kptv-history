Kurt Ludlow

Kurt Ludlow returns to the WBNS-10TV anchor desk, and joins 10TV anchor/reporter Ellie Merritt to anchor the weekend editions of 10TV Eyewitness News at 6 & 11.

Ludlow spent 13 years at WBNS-10TV beginning in 1986 as a general assignment reporter. During his tenure, he won numerous awards, including regional Emmy awards and the prestigious Edward R. Murrow award for a ground breaking documentary titled "Inside the Fire," a captivating look at the dynamics of house fires from the inside out.

Ludlow ascended to the weekend anchor desk and later helped create 10TV's award-winning investigative unit. He left WBNS in 1999 accepting the primary anchor position at KPTV in Portland, Oregon. While there, he again won numerous awards and helped lead the #5 rated KPTV to #1 in the market.

Ludlow graduated from the University of Colorado at Boulder and later received his law degree from Capital University in Columbus, Ohio. Ludlow is active in numerous charities and is the proud father of twin girls. Kurt's wife Laura is a native of Ohio.

"I have spent nearly one-third of my life in Columbus," said Ludlow. "So in most every way it feels like home. I'm excited to get back to my family and my friends and look forward to getting involved again in the community."
