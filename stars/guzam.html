Monica Guza

Monica Guza is a native of the Pacific Northwest, born in Seattle, and raised in Eugene. After a particularly rainy summer, she fled to Northern California...but Oregon called her back. She majored in film and video production in college in hopes of working in documentary film. Instead, she got an offer to work as a weathercaster at KMTR in Eugene.

She joined the KPTV-Oregon's 12 in February 2000, and is the primary weather anchor on the weekend editions of The 10 O'Clock News. Monica and her husband live outside of town with their menagerie of cats, birds, and one lovable mutt. For fun, Monica strives to keep 40 mature rosebushes alive, rides motorcycles, and skis on both snow and water. You might also find her lending a critical eye to the latest film, or planning her next tropical vacation.
