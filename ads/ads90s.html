KPTV Ad Museum/1990-1999

A look back at print advertising from newspapers, magazines, flyers, posters and TV Guide.

The Oregonian, May 10, 1990
Several years before it became a weekly series, "Northwest Reports" was a series of specials. Lars Larson established the potential of this local newsmagazine, with a thoughtful and comprehensive look at the ten years following the Northwest's most famous disaster.

 

1997 Rose Festival Souvenir Guide
As one of the sponsors of Portland's annual Rose Festival, KPTV bought this full-page ad on the inside cover of their souvenir guide. Featuring the casts of KPTV's morning and evening news show, this ad prominently displayed Channel 12's new Eastside residence on the Willamette River.
