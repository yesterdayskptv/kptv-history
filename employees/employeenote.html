Employee Section



In order to protect the privacy of KPTV's employees, we have removed the Employee section that was available in 2003. If you are a former KPTV employee (during the years 1952-2002) and would like to be notified about reunions, periodic lunch get-togethers, or historical station information, please email yesterdayskptv@gmail.com. Include your name, years with the station, position(s) held, and an email address or phone number where you may be contacted.

Thank you.  --Ron Dunevant, webmaster
