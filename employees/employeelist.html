KPTV Employee List
Below is a list of KPTV employees gathered from phone lists, documents and newspaper and magazine articles.  Currently, there are over 750 names of people who have worked at KPTV during its first 50 years. Names are alphabetical by surname. When names changed (due to marriages, etc.) the main information is attached to the name as it first appeared in our sources of information. Subsequent names will refer you to the initial name.
INFORMATION IN YELLOW INDICATES WHAT WE BELIEVE TO BE THE ACTUAL DATE.
Please note that start and end dates listed have been gathered from phone extension lists, address lists, magazine articles and other sources.  The dates represent the first and last time the name appears on said list, and may not be anywhere near the actual dates of employment.  If you have additional information on any person listed, or if you know of someone who is not listed, please send your information to the webmaster.  Thank you.
