KPTV Reunion 2006!

It's that time of year again….time for another KPTV Alumni Get Together!  Here’s the information:

KPTV Alumni Reunion
Saturday May 20, 2006; 4:00pm-10:00pm
The Kingston Saloon
2021 SW Morrison Street
(across W. Burnside from Stadium Fred Meyer)
503.224.2115

(Please note this get-together is for former employees of both the SW 20th Place and Caruthers Street stations, before the merger with Meredith Corp.)

I think the best compliment I received last year was from our late friend Margo Chambers.  She was so happy and excited to see everyone and was just simply bursting with joy with all the familiar faces.  Her happiness that night will forever be my motivating factor for continuing with our tradition. We started this as an opportunity to say thanks and farewell to some of the folks that we were so fortunate to work with and I would like to see this continue as an opportunity for us to keep in touch, reconnect, network and support all the wonderful people we were so lucky to cross paths with.  

If you have a moment, let us know what you are up to, where we can find you, if you are open to networking with former employees, etc.  We will post all responses we receive in the Employees section so check back from time to time to see what's new!

A few of you may be asking….

Why the Kingston again?

Well first and foremost, Gary is the Host with the Most.  He allows us to reserve the bar without any rental fee, food or beverage minimum, allows us to run separate tabs and is phenomenally accommodating to any request we have, including a few pots of Tamale Pie Soup.  I have no contract to sign or credit card requested to secure payment.  I realize that parking may be a challenge, especially as the evening progresses, so my recommendation would be to get there early, carpool, ask a friend or family member to drop you off or possibly take a cab.  If you have any other ideas for a different venue with similar terms and conditions by all means let me know!  I will consider any opportunity that you may recommend, although it will not change where we are meeting for this year's Reunion since I have committed to Gary that we will be there. 

Did you email or contact this person?

Ron and I have done our best at maintaining the email database.  For those of you who send us updated information…thank you!  If you are in contact with formers co-workers…ask them!  I would rather they be asked three or four times than to never hear about it at all! Take the initiative….JUST DO IT!!!! and help us out.  We would greatly appreciate it.  Please forward them the email and cc the Alumni address (yesterdayskptv@gmail.com) so we can add them to our database. If they do not have email please feel call them or mail them a copy of the invitation. 

We are so very fortunate to have someone as passionate as Ron Dunevant to maintain our beautiful website and rigorously maintain our database. Ron and I do this little gathering on our own time and our own dime. We do not have a budget for sending out invitations or making long distance phone calls so anything you can do to help us spread the news is greatly appreciated.  Since I do not work for KPTV I do not feel that it's appropriate for me to use my work time to handle Alumni business so I am asking for your help in making this as successful this year as it has been in years gone by.

A few requests:

RSVP! Call 503-232-9053 or reply to this email with a yes or no.  This is so we can insure that we have enough servers for our area.

JUST DO IT and take the initiative to contact someone…better they hear it twice than never.

SHARE THE NEWS forward the email, make a phone call, send it snail mail.

CHECK OUR M.I.A. LIST after this e-mail is sent, we will post a list of “bounced” messages and friends we have lost contact with. Go to the employee section of the Yesterday’s KPTV site, www.kptv.org or click here. Username: employee (the word “employee”, not your name) Password: bigonetwo (as in “the big 1-2”)

KEEP IN TOUCH Let us know what you've been up to and where we can reach you if you're up for a little networking.

HAVE FUN!  I can't wait to see everyone on May 20th.

Cheers,

Lori Jensen

P.S. If you or a fellow KPTV alum would like to host a get-together, we will help you get the word out. Just send us a note to yesterdayskptv@gmail.com, along with your message, and we will pass it along.
